Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi friend rice is a popular dish in South Korea. It is a variation of bokkeum-bap (fried rice) that is primarily kimchi and rice along with other vegetables and meats of choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1/4 cup ground beef
  • 1 green onion, sliced, white and green parts separated
  • 1 cup well-fermented kimchi, drained and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang
  • 3 cups cooked short-grain rice
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon buttermilk
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Heat the canola oil in a large nonstick skillet over high heat. Add ground beef and white parts of green onion; reduce heat to medium.
  2. Cook and stir beef and green onion until meat is browned and onion is fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Stir kimchi and gochujang into meat mixture and cook until warmed through and fragrant, 2 to 4 minutes.
  4. Add rice; cook and stir until rice is heated through and coated with gochujang, 3 to 5 minutes more.
  5. Drizzle sesame oil over rice mixture and stir to coat. Garnish with green parts of green onion.
  6. Melt 1 teaspoon butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Crack egg into the pan and cook until white is completely set and yolk is thick, 3 to 4 minutes. Place egg over fried rice.
